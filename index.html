Origin of goods

Sea Sun's products originate from the islands of Cam Ranh, Nha Trang and some other seas from Phan Thiet, Phu Quoc and are imported from countries such as the US, Canada, Japan, Korea, Norway, Denmark, etc. The products we provide you are 100% natural, no preservatives. Seafood is imported and supplied to customers according to a monitoring process to ensure the freshest and best quality seafood to customers.
